About Us

Wild Circus was born in 2016 thanks to the investment of the WILD CODE SCHOOL.
Since, the number of performances continues to grow to enchant a maximum of children and adults around the world.
The show includes flying trapeze, an extraordinary parade of elephants, equestrian accrobatics and an unforgettable show of tigers and lions that will amaze you. Obviously, many hilarious clowns put their good humor at the service of the public.
WILD Circus is the show to see in 2018
